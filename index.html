What Makes a Boardify Wallboard Different From a Normal Photo Frame?

Traditional glass and wooden photo frames belong in the past. They are heavy, fragile, expensive, and require a hammer and nails to hang. Here is exactly why switching to a Boardify wallboard is the ultimate upgrade for your home:

01

Premium Material & Structure

Unlike basic paper prints crammed into wooden frames, every Boardify wallboard is crafted using a high-density, 3mm premium PVC board. This gives each piece a lightweight, perfectly flat, and ultra-sleek matte profile that sits flawlessly against your wall.

02

100% Waterproof & Dustproof

Normal frames trap moisture, warp over time, and accumulate dust behind the glass. Our boards feature a specialized protective lamination that makes them completely waterproof and dustproof. If they ever get dirty, you can simply wipe them clean with a damp cloth without risking any color fading or water damage. Lifetime color vibrancy is guaranteed.

03

Zero Drilling & Wall Damage

To hang a normal frame, you have to damage your wall with a drill or hammer nails into the brick, leaving ugly holes behind. Boardify wallboards are engineered to be entirely drill-free.

04

Complimentary Mounting Tape

Every single board you order comes with pre-applied, industrial-grade foam mounting tape for free. All you have to do is peel the backing off and press the board firmly onto your wall. It takes less than 30 seconds to install, and stays securely in place for years.
